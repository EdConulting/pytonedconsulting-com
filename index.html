    About Payton Education Consulting

    I’ve been in TK-12 education for 10 years. In that time, I've worked in operational roles, spanning the breadth of HR functions, including credentialing in California and Washington State, HR Management, DEI, and compliance. I believe schools and districts deserve to know their credential and HR risks and get support in managing them, as well as improving hiring through high quality DEI and hiring practices. This is key to best serving school communities by reducing risk and freeing school staff and faculty to concentrate on their students.
